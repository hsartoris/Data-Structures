Show $7n^2 + 5n -1$ is $\O(n^2)$\\
Need to prove $7n^2 + 5n -1 \leq Cn^2$ for $C>0, n>n_0$\\
\begin{align*}
7n^2 &\leq 7n^2\\
5n &\leq 5n^2 \tag{if $n \geq 1$} \\
-1 &\leq 0 \\
7n^2 + 5n -1 &\leq 7n^2 + 5n^2 \\
&\leq 12n^2 = Cn^2\\
\end{align*}
